iPoem

iPoem is an innovative web application that merges artificial intelligence with creative music generation to craft a unique fusion of poetry and sound. At its heart, iPoem uses cutting-edge AI models to interpret emotions and moods, transforming them into evocative poems. But it doesn’t stop there—each poem is accompanied by a custom-generated piece of music, carefully composed to reflect the rhythm of the words and the chosen genre.

Milestone 1: Initial Development and AI Integration

Start your journey into AI-driven creativity with Milestone 1. This phase focuses on establishing the foundational technology stack and integrating advanced AI models to transform moods into expressive, creative poems. Once the poetic content is generated, the models take it a step further, converting the emotional nuances of the poem into a short, mood-aligned piece of music.

  • Mood-to-Poem: Leverage Hugging Face’s AI model to analyze user input and transform emotional cues into expressive, personalized poems.
  • Poem-to-Music: Use an AI-driven music generation model to compose short musical pieces based on the tone, mood, and rhythm of the generated poem.
  • Framework: Developed with Flask and styled with Bootstrap to ensure a responsive and visually appealing interface that enhances user interaction.

Milestone 2: Advanced Features and Enhanced Interactivity

Take your creative journey to the next level with Milestone 2, which introduces advanced interactivity and theme customization. This phase allows users to randomly generate a mood and enter a theme, giving more control over the emotional tone and style of the generated poems and music. Experience personalized poetic and musical expressions that resonate with your selected or randomly generated mood.

  • Random Mood Generation: Users can generate a random mood, which the AI uses as the foundation for creating a themed poem and matching musical composition.
  • Theme Selection: Choose from a variety of themes, allowing for more tailored poetic and musical creations that align with user preferences.
  • Mood-Driven Visuals and Sounds: Both the visual presentation and musical output adapt dynamically to the selected mood or theme, enhancing the overall immersive experience.
